Personal

I was born in Qingdao City, located on the east coast of China, Asia. My childhood is closely connected with the beach and the sea, where I spent much of my outdoor time. I love swimming and enjoyed the sea waves, which likely explains my passion for fluid dynamics.

Perhaps because my hometown has many German buildings, and due to my days and days of work on "beach town construction," I am also interested in architecture, especially different architectural styles and structures. I enjoy sketching and building miniature models of these designs. This could also explain why I chose a path that combines mathematics and mechanics: without them, nothing can stand or last long, not even the smallest structures.
